Hotel Information

Walkerhill Hotel

Surrounded by beautiful Han River and green Achasan, the Walkerhill Hotel offers a variety of cultural experiences
as well as customers who want to get away from the hustle and bustle of city life with the four seasons that can enjoy spring cherry blossoms,
summer recordings, autumn leaves and winter snow. It is an ideal place for your desired customers.
In particular, the hotel has various facilities such as an outdoor pool river park with views of the Han River,
a camping zone where you can enjoy a unique camping experience, and a kids club.
