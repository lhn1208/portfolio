Lotte World

See the list

Lotte world is a theme park established and operated by Lotte Group to leisure of the national people and attraction of foreign tourists. It is an indoor theme park, a large complex living space with adventure and mystery themed theme, Lotte World Adventure, Lake Park Magic Island, shopping mall, folk museum, ice rink, hotel, department store, etc. to solve tourism, leisure, shopping and culture in one place. Lotte World Adventure offers a state-of-the-art boarding facility, fantastic parades, video systems, laser shows, performances, and national food all year round. Lotte World Adventure is the largest indoor theme park in the world. The folk museum is divided into history museum, folk museum, playground, and author distance. Lotte World Garden Stage is the center stage of Lotte World performance, and various events such as musical shows and public broadcasting are held in various seasons. Lotte World Star Avenue is an experience-based entertainment facility with a Korean-style star street.
