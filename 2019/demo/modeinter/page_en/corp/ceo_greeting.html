Message
In the past 20 years I started to serve the
Korean people with the motto of
'Fresh joy and romance of overseas travel'...
Two decades ago, Modetour set about its endeavor to realize its motto of l'providing the fresh joy and pleasure of overseas travel' to Koreans. Now, beyond its accomplish ment it enjoys with the reputation of the real travel agency of Korea, Modetour is challenging another goal that is, development into a global enterprise. In this context, it establishes ModetourInternational.

Thanks to all the customers, executives, employees, shareholders, and affiliates that have encouraged Modetour to grow, Modetour International and Modetour Network is now ready, able, and willing to provide Chinese customers with qualified tourism service and more tour opportunities in Korea.

Modetour International and Modetour Network, expanding their customer base all over the world and taking advantage of the reputation, expertise, and passion Modetour has accumulated, will do their best to offer the joy and pleasure of travel, whether inbound or outbound. They are ready to run for a brighter future based on the past success of Modetour.

The tourism industry is one of the most valuable growth engines of Korea. Korea has priceless tourism resources – its proud 5,000-year history and culture – and unfor- tunately, they have not been fully utilized yet. There is more. The Korean Wave, which is spreading from Asia to the entire world, and the unique ‘sentiment’ a racially homogeneous nation can has – they are the intangible tourism resources of Korea, second to none in attraction and competitiveness.

Now, Modetour International, the harbinger of the tourism industry of Korea, will provide tourism service for people around the world. And it will successfully accomplish the goal of developing Korea into a major tourism power as well as growing itself and Modetour Network into real leading players in global tourism.

Thank you
Ho Kwon Son, CEO of Modetour International
